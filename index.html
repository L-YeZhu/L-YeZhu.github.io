Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology, USA. I work under the supervision of Dr. Yan Yan in the research area of computer vision with focuses on multimodal learning (vision, audio and languages). Throughout my graduate studies, I have interned several times in industry, including Snap Inc. at USA, Bang & Olufsen A/S at Denmark.

I recieved my B.S. and M.S. degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2016 and 2019, respectively. I also studied at Ecole Polytechnique in France and got my French Engineering diploma in 2019.

I serve as a reviewer for CVPR, ECCV, IEEE Transactions on Multimedia, ACM Multimedia, ICASSP, Neurocomputing and etc. In addition to the academic work, I am a French translator for the European scientific magzine Science & Vie.
