Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the department of Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology, USA. I work under the supervision of Dr. Yan Yan. My main research interests are computer vision with focuses on multimodal learning (vision, audio and languages). I recieved my B.S. and M.S. degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2016 and 2019, respectively. I also studied at Ecole Polytechnique in France and got my French Engineering diploma in 2019. During my stay in Europe, I also interned in the research team at Bang & Olufsen A/S at Denmark.
